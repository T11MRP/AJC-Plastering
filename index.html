What we do

Every surface, taken to a finish

Plastering is the core of what we do, but we handle the whole job — from bare walls and ceilings through to render, boarding and the full renovation around it.

Most requested

Skimming & plastering

Fresh skim coats over old walls, ceilings and new boards — flattened and polished to a smooth, ready-to-decorate finish with no ridges or trowel lines.

External rendering

Weatherproof render that lifts the whole look of a property — even coats, crisp edges and a durable finish built to handle the British weather.

Plasterboarding & dry lining

Boarding out walls, ceilings and loft rooms ready for skimming — including reveals, skylight returns and the tricky angles that need a careful eye.

Ceilings & coving

Overboarding and re-skimming tired ceilings, plus clean returns around downlights, skylights and vaulted spaces so the finish reads as one surface.

Renovations & refurbishment

Full-room and whole-property refurbishment — from stripping back and prep through plaster, render and the finishing work that pulls a space together.

Patch & repair

Cracks, blown plaster, damp-damaged walls and patch repairs made good and blended in, so the fix disappears into the wall around it.

Why work with us

A finish that speaks for itself

The difference between a good plaster job and a bad one only shows once the paint goes on. We work to get it right at the trowel, not to hide it afterwards.

  • Turn up when we sayStraight answers on timing and price, and we're there when we tell you we will be. No chasing, no vanishing mid-job.
